# Privacy Policy Source Notes

## Current release behavior

Turbo Chess v1 is free, ad-free, offline-focused, and local-only.

Current Android release behavior:

* No ads
* No analytics
* No crash reporting
* No login
* No account creation
* No cloud sync
* No in-app purchases
* No subscriptions
* No Google Play Billing
* No release `INTERNET` permission
* No location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or storage permission requested by the Android release app

## Local data

Turbo Chess stores progress locally on the device. Local data can include completed positions, bookmarks, game history, active game state, settings, sound/haptic choices, and engine settings.

Local app data is not sent to developer servers by the current Android release app. Clearing app data or uninstalling the app may delete local data.

## Privacy policy draft points

A privacy policy for the current release should explain that Turbo Chess is free and ad-free, does not require an account, stores progress/settings locally, does not send local progress to developer servers, and does not request internet access in the Android release build.

Third-party notices are available in More > Legal.

A public contact email should be included in the hosted privacy policy.
