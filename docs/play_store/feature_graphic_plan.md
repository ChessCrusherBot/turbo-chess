# Feature Graphic Plan

## Required format

* Size: 1024 x 500 px
* Format: PNG or JPEG
* Recommended export: 24-bit PNG or JPEG
* Transparency: avoid transparent backgrounds for the feature graphic

## Design direction

Use the Turbo Chess visual style, the app icon or brand mark, and a clear chess-board or tactical-position visual. Short text such as "Offline Chess Training" or "30,000 Chess Drills" fits the current app state.

## Store listing wording notes

The feature graphic should avoid third-party logos, Google Play badges, fake awards, unverifiable ranking claims, permanence claims, and copyrighted third-party artwork.
