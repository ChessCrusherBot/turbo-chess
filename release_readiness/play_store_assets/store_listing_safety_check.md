# Turbo Chess Store Listing Safety Check

Checklist:

* App title under limit.
* Short description under 80 characters.
* Full description honest.
* No misleading ranking claims.
* No fake awards.
* No third-party brand comparisons.
* No old ads/subscription/rewarded-pass wording.
* No privacy mismatch.
* No screenshots from old builds.
* No debug UI.
* No copyrighted third-party logos.
* No claims that Stockfish/Lichess officially endorse the app.
* No statement that app is official Stockfish or official Lichess.
