# Turbo Chess Privacy Policy Source Notes

Current release behavior:

* No ads.
* No AdMob.
* No analytics.
* No Crashlytics.
* No login.
* No account creation.
* No cloud sync.
* No in-app purchases.
* No subscriptions.
* No Google Play Billing.
* No release INTERNET permission.
* No location/camera/microphone/contacts/photos/storage permission.
* App stores progress locally on the device.
* Local data may include completed positions, bookmarks, game history, active game state, settings, sound/haptic choices, and engine settings.
* Local data is not sent to developer servers.
* Clearing app data or uninstalling the app may delete local data.

Privacy policy later should say:

* Turbo Chess is free and ad-free.
* Turbo Chess does not require an account.
* Turbo Chess stores progress/settings locally.
* Turbo Chess does not send local progress to developer servers.
* Turbo Chess does not request internet access in the Android release build.
* Third-party notices are available in More > Legal.
* Contact email must be added before publishing.
