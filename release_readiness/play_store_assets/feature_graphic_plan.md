# Turbo Chess Feature Graphic Plan

Required size:
1024 x 500 px

Format:
PNG or JPEG. Use 24-bit PNG or JPEG. Avoid transparency for the feature graphic.

Design direction:

* Dark Turbo Chess theme.
* App icon/brand on one side.
* Chess board or tactical position in the center.
* Short simple text such as:
  "Offline Chess Training"
  or
  "30,000 Chess Drills"
* Keep the design clean and not crowded.
* Use Turbo Chess colors and branding.

Avoid:

* Chess.com logo
* Lichess logo
* Stockfish logo unless permission/brand rules are confirmed
* Google Play badge
* "Best chess app"
* "#1"
* "Free forever"
* misleading trophies/awards
* copyrighted third-party artwork
