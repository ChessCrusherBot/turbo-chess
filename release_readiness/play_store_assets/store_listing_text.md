## Full description

Turbo Chess is a free, ad-free chess training app for offline practice.

Train with 30,000 bundled chess positions across Opening, Middlegame, and Endgame drills. Open any position, play from the position against the engine, bookmark useful drills, and track your progress locally on your device.

You can also play against the computer, save unfinished games, review game history, and replay saved games move by move.

Turbo Chess is designed for simple offline chess practice. It does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not use in-app purchases.

Features:

* 30,000 bundled training positions
* Opening, Middlegame, and Endgame drills
* All positions unlocked
* Play positions against the engine
* Play vs Computer
* Game history and replay
* Bookmarks
* Local progress saving
* Sound and haptic settings
* Offline-friendly Android release
* No account required
* Free and ad-free

Turbo Chess includes third-party open-source components and assets. Legal and open-source notices are available inside the app under More > Legal.
